# Palette's Journal

This journal records critical UX and accessibility learnings from the field.

## 2024-05-22 - The "Sticky" Info Card
**Learning:** Floating information cards (like territory details) that appear on selection feel "sticky" and frustrating if they can't be explicitly dismissed without deselecting.
**Action:** Always include a clear, accessible "Close" button on floating panels, even if clicking outside also works.
